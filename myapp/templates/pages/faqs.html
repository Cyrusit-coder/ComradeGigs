For Students

This platform is exclusive to university students. Uploading your ID proves you are a verified comrade, which builds trust with clients who hire you.

Payment is sent directly to your registered M-Pesa number immediately after the client marks the job as complete.

To maintain the platform, we deduct a small 5% service fee from your earnings.
(e.g., If a gig pays Ksh 1,000, you receive Ksh 950).
