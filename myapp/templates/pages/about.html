The Spark

Why We Started

It started with a simple observation: University hostels are full of talented graphic designers, writers, coders, and virtual assistants who are broke simply because they lack a platform to showcase their work.

ComradeGigs was born to fix this. We replaced the "tarmacking" mentality with a digital marketplace where meritocracy rules. If you have the skill, we have the gig.
