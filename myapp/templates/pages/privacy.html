Privacy Policy

Your data security is our priority.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Name, Email address, and Password.
  • Verification Data: University/College ID images (for Students only).
  • Financial Data: M-Pesa phone numbers for processing payments.
  • Profile Data: Skills, education history, and portfolio uploads.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To verify that students are legitimate university enrollees.
  • To process secure M-Pesa payments between Clients, Donors, and Students.
  • To match students with relevant gigs based on their skills.
  • To improve platform security and detect fraud.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your School ID is strictly confidential; it is visible only to our Admin verification team and is never shared with Clients or third parties.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share necessary data with:
- Safaricom (M-Pesa): To facilitate transaction processing.
- Cloud Storage Providers: To securely store your uploaded documents.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can edit your profile directly from your dashboard. To request permanent account deletion, contact support.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or dashboard notifications.
