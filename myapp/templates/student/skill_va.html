Skill Module: Virtual Assistant

Admin & Organization Assessment

Prove your organizational skills. Learn how to handle professional emails, data entry, and research for busy clients.

Quick Study Guide

1. Email Etiquette

Always start with a professional greeting ("Dear [Name]"). Be concise. Use bullet points for tasks. End with a clear call to action and a "Best Regards".

2. Google Suite Mastery

Docs: For notes/writing. Sheets: For data entry. Calendar: For scheduling. Always check sharing permissions (Viewer vs Editor) before sending links.

3. Calendar Management

When scheduling, always confirm the TimeZone (e.g., EST, GMT, EAT). Send actual calendar invites, not just text confirmations.

4. Internet Research

Clients pay for summarized info, not raw links. If asked to find "Top 5 Laptops", provide a clean spreadsheet comparing Price, Specs, and Reviews.
