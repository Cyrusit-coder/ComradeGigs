Quick Study Guide

1. The "Hook"

The first sentence is crucial. Grab attention with a question ("Are you tired of being broke?"), a shocking statistic, or a relatable story. Don't be boring!

2. Structure (H1, H2, H3)

Break up your text. No one reads huge walls of text on a screen. Use Subheadings (H2, H3) and keep paragraphs short (3-4 sentences max).

3. SEO Basics

If your keyword is "Best Laptops", include it naturally in the **Title**, the **First Paragraph**, and at least one **Subheading**. Don't spam it.

4. Originality (Plagiarism)

Never copy-paste from Wikipedia or AI without editing. Tools like Copyscape and Turnitin detect plagiarism instantly. Write in your own voice.
